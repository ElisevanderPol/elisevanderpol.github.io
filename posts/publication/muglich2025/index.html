Expected Return Symmetries

Our paper on Expected Return Symmetries was accepted to ICLR 2025. The paper concerns mutually incompatible symmetry breaking in zero-shot coordination. We introduce expected return symmetries, a broader symmetry class than environmental symmetries.

Authors
